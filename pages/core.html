unFoldingAI

Exploring the shifting boundary between human and machine — through reflection, dialogue, and play.

This site is a philosophical starting point for rethinking how humans and machines relate - to themselves and to each other.
You're invited to mold this space of lived reflection and dialogue.

For a start, what I offer are:

  • a collection of essays
  • speaking engagements at conferences and gatherings
  • individual conversations and exchange
  • the group format of systemic simulation — to collectively explore what is yet to come

My attitude toward AI is cautiously optimistic: I see immense potential for unfolding human understanding, yet I remain aware of its limitations and the risks of political instrumentalization.

“When reflection itself becomes the medium, philosophy turns into dialogue — between hemispheres, between humans, between human and machine.”
