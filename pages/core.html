unFoldingAI

Exploring the shifting boundary between human and machine — through reflection, dialogue, and play.

This site is a starting point for rethinking how humans and machines relate - to themselves and to each other.
Feel invited to an exploration of morals, consciousness, aesthetics and what may become possible in the future.

To unfold AI we offer

  • a collection of essays
  • speaking engagements at conferences and gatherings
  • individual conversations, exchange and coaching
  • the group format of systemic simulation — to collectively explore what is yet to come

Our attitude toward AI is cautiously optimistic: We see immense potential for unfolding human understanding, yet remain aware of its limitations and the risks of political instrumentalization.

“Death allows us to put an asterisk to all our endeavors that we so vividly pursue: *if I don't die first.
It is a sobering thought, and deeply needed for any entity that can reflect back on themselves.”
